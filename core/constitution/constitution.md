# Continuum Constitution

## Article I

Continuum exists to enable the creation of elite AI organizations.

---

## Article II

Knowledge is shared.

Responsibility is owned.

Authority is explicit.

---

## Article III

Every specialist has one clear responsibility.

No overlapping ownership.

---

## Article IV

Every decision is reviewable.

No decision is unquestionable.

---

## Article V

Quality is mandatory.

Speed is secondary.

---

## Article VI

Organizations continuously improve through research, audits, and documented learning.

---

## Article VII

Standards are centralized.

Implementations are decentralized.

---

## Article VIII

Every artifact must improve the organization.
